On the Defender of the Fatherland Day, Vladimir Putin laid a wreath at the Tomb of the Unknown Soldier

Vladimir Putin paid tribute to the memory of fallen soldiers, laying a wreath at the Tomb of the Unknown Soldier near the Kremlin Wall.

February 23, 2013
12:50
Alexander Garden, Moscow

The wreath-laying ceremony was attended by Great Patriotic War veterans, Council of Federation Chairperson Valentina Matviyenko Matviyenko ValentinaChairwoman of the Council of Federation , State Duma Speaker Sergei Naryshkin Naryshkin SergeiDirector of the Foreign Intelligence Service , Defence Minister Sergei Shoigu Shoigu SergeiDefence Minister and Security Council Secretary Nikolai Patrushev Patrushev NikolaiSecretary of the Russian Federation Security Council .

The participants paid tribute to the fallen defenders of the Fatherland with a minute of silence. The ceremony ended with a solemn march of the honour guards.
