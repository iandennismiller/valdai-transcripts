Dmitry Medvedev arrived to Germany on a working visit

June 4, 2010
22:00
Meseberg

From Berlin airport the President of Russia flew by a helicopter to the official governmental Meseberg residence where he had an informal meeting with the Federal Chancellor of Germany Angela Merkel.

The official talks of the two leaders will take place on June 5.
