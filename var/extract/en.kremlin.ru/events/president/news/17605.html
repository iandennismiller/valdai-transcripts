Trip to Pskov

Vladimir Putin visited the 76th Guards Air Assault Division and took part in events commemorating the memory of soldiers from the 6th Paratroopers Company killed in action.

March 1, 2013
18:30
Pskov

The President laid flowers at a memorial to the paratroopers who died heroically in combat missions in the North Caucasus in 2000, and also spoke later at an evening organised in their memory.

The President also inspected the troops of the 104th Guards Air Assault Regiment and met with paratroopers commanders.
