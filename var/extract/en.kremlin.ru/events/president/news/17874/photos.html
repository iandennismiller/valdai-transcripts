Trip to Siberian Federal District

Vladimir Putin visited Ivolga Datsan in Buryatia and several timber companies in the Trans-Baikal Territory. Later the President chaired a meeting of the State Council Presidium on strengthening the timber sector.

April 11, 2013
12:00
Bada, Verkhnyaya Ivolga, Ulan-Ude
