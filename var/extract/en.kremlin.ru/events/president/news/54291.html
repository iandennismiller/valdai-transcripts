Meeting of the Supreme Eurasian Economic Council

A meeting of the Supreme Eurasian Economic Council was held in Bishkek.

April 14, 2017
12:20
Bishkek

The Supreme Eurasian Economic Council meeting in narrow format was attended by President of Russia Vladimir Putin, President of Kyrgyzstan Almazbek Atambayev Atambayev Almazbek , President of Belarus Alexander Lukashenko Lukashenko AlexanderPresident of Belarus , President of Kazakhstan Nursultan Nazarbayev Nazarbayev Nursultan and President of Armenia Serzh Sargsyan Sargsyan Serzh .

They discussed the Supreme Eurasian Economic Council’s performance in 2016, its further development and the main macroeconomic priorities of the Eurasian Economic Union (EAEU) member states for the next few years.

A package of documents was signed following the meeting, including a decision to grant the status of EAEU observer state to Moldova.

Vladimir Putin also had a number of bilateral meetings on the sidelines of the Eurasian Economic Union and the Collective Security Treaty Organisation summit meetings in Bishkek. He met, in particular, with President of Tajikistan Emomali Rahmon Rahmon EmomaliPresident of Tajikistan and President of Kyrgyzstan Almazbek Atambayev.
