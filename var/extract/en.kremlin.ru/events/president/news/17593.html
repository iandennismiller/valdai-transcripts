Meeting with President of France Francois Hollande

Vladimir Putin and President of France Francois Hollande held talks in the Kremlin.

February 28, 2013
18:00
The Kremlin, Moscow

During a meeting in restricted format, the two leaders discussed key aspects of bilateral cooperation in trade, energy, transport, investments, and the high-tech sector, as well as humanitarian cooperation. Mr Putin and Mr Hollande Hollande Francois also exchanged opinions on current international issues.

Following their talks, the two leaders witnessed the signing of a number of bilateral documents.

Vladimir Putin and Francois Hollande also gave a joint news conference.
