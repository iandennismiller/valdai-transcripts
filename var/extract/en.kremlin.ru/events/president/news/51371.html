Meeting on economic issues

The President held a meeting to discuss the plan to support the Russian economy in 2016.

February 20, 2016
16:40
The Kremlin, Moscow
Meeting on economic issues.

On the instructions of Dmitry Medvedev Medvedev DmitryDeputy Chairman of the Security Council of the Russian Federation , the heads of the Cabinet’s economic block briefed the President on the details of the action plan the Government is currently working on to ensure Russia’s stable social and economic development in 2016.

Taking part in the meeting were First Deputy Prime Minister Igor Shuvalov Shuvalov IgorChairman of the State Development Corporation VEB.RF , Deputy Prime Minister Olga Golodets Golodets Olga , Finance Minister Anton Siluanov Siluanov AntonFinance Minister , Central Bank Governor Elvira Nabiullina Nabiullina ElviraGovernor of the Central Bank of the Russian Federation (Bank of Russia) , Accounts Chamber Chairperson Tatyana Golikova Golikova TatyanaDeputy Prime Minister and Aide to the President Andrei Belousov Belousov AndreiFirst Deputy Prime Minister .
