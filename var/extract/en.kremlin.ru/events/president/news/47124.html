Meeting with President of Turkey Recep Tayyip Erdogan

Vladimir Putin met with President of Turkey Recep Tayyip Erdogan while on a state visit to Turkey.

December 1, 2014
18:00
Ankara

The meeting was preceded by an official welcome ceremony and presentation of the delegations.

Earlier, Mr Putin laid a wreath at the tomb of Mustafa Kemal Ataturk to honour the memory of the Turkish Republic’s first president.
