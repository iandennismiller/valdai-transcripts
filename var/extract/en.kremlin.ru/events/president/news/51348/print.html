Meeting with Hungarian Prime Minister Viktor Orban

Vladimir Putin met with Prime Minister of Hungary Viktor Orban at his residence outside Moscow. Mr Orban is in Russia on a working visit.

February 17, 2016
17:50
Novo-Ogaryovo, Moscow Region

Mr Putin and Mr Orban Orban ViktorPrime Minister of Hungary discussed opportunities for further developing bilateral cooperation and exchanged views on current international issues.

Following the talks, Mr Putin and Mr Orban made press statements and answered journalists’ questions.
