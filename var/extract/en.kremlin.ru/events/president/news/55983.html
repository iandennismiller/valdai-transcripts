Meeting of leaders of Russia, Iran and Azerbaijan

Tehran hosted a trilateral meeting between Vladimir Putin, President of Iran Hassan Rouhani and President of Azerbaijan Ilham Aliyev.

November 1, 2017
19:30
Tehran

Following the summit, the leaders of Russia, Iran and Azerbaijan adopted a joint statement.

After their meeting, Vladimir Putin, Hassan Rouhani Rouhani HassanPresident of Iran and Ilham Aliyev Aliyev IlhamPresident of Azerbaijan made press statements.

Earlier in the day, as part of his working visit to Iran, Vladimir Putin met separately with the heads of Iran and Azerbaijan.
