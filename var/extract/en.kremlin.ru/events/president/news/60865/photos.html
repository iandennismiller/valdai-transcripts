Visit to Republic of Belarus

During his working visit to the Republic of Belarus, the President of Russia attended boxing and artistic gymnastics competitions held as part of the 2nd European Games, met with President of the International Olympic Committee Thomas Bach, and spoke with Russian athletes who are taking part in the Games. In addition, Vladimir Putin had an informal meeting with President of Belarus Alexander Lukashenko and President of Tajikistan Emomali Rahmon. At the end of his visit, the President of Russia attended the closing ceremony of the 2nd European Games.
