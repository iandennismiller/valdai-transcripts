Meeting with permanent members of Security Council

Vladimir Putin chaired a briefing meeting with permanent members of the Security Council.

June 26, 2019
17:50
The Kremlin, Moscow

The participants exchanged opinions in the context of preparations for the upcoming G20 summit in Osaka as well as the bilateral contacts on its sidelines.
