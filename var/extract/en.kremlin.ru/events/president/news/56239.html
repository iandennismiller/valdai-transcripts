Visiting the Moscow Kievskaya depot

Vladimir Putin visited the train service depot at the Moscow Kievskaya station.

November 29, 2017
15:00
Moscow

The President was updated on the operation of the Russian Railway’s passenger branch and shown around the passenger trains on display.

Mr Putin also talked with the depot staff who maintain and operate passenger trains.

The Moscow Kievskaya depot provides maintenance services to long-distance trains, including trains running to the CIS and other countries.
