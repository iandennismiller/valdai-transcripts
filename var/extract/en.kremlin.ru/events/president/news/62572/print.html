Meeting on drafting Address to Federal Assembly

Vladimir Putin held one of a series of working meetings on the drafting of the Address to the Federal Assembly that will take place on January 15.

January 13, 2020
15:10
The Kremlin, Moscow

The participants discussed a broad range of issues, paying special attention to individual matters that required additional analysis.
