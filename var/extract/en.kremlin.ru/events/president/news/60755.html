Vladimir Putin arrived in Dushanbe

June 14, 2019
18:30
Dushanbe

The President of Russia arrived in the capital of Tajikistan on a two-day working visit to take part in the summit of the Conference on Interaction and Confidence-Building Measures in Asia (CICA).

President of Tajikistan Emomali Rahmon Rahmon EmomaliPresident of Tajikistan met Vladimir Putin as he came off the plane. The leaders of the two countries had a brief conversation at the airport. Later tonight, Vladimir Putin will also attend an informal dinner on behalf of the President of Tajikistan in honour of the heads of state taking part in the Conference on Interaction and Confidence-Building Measures in Asia (CICA). The main events will take place tomorrow.

At the CICA plenary session, the participants plan to discuss issues related to ensuring regional security, countering new challenges and threats, developing economic cooperation and integration processes, and expanding humanitarian cooperation.

The participants will also exchange views on the developments in Afghanistan, Syria, Iran and on the Korean Peninsula.

In addition, Vladimir Putin may hold separate meetings with foreign leaders.

The CICA summit will be attended by delegations from 27 member countries and 13 observers, including representatives from international organisations such as the UN, the League of Arab States Background information League of Arab States (LAS) , the OSCE Background information Organization for Security and Co-operation in Europe (OSCE) and the Parliamentary Assembly of Turkic-Speaking Countries.
