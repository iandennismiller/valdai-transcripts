Meetings with Serzh Sargsyan, Alexander Lukashenko and Nursultan Nazarbayev

December 19, 2012
21:30
The Kremlin, Moscow

Upon the completion of CSTO Background information Collective Security Treaty Organisation (CSTO) and EurAsEC Background information Eurasian Economic Community (EurAsEC) summits and the Supreme Eurasian Economic Council meeting, Vladimir Putin held bilateral meetings with President of Armenia Serzh Sargsyan Sargsyan Serzh , President of Belarus Alexander Lukashenko Lukashenko AlexanderPresident of Belarus and President of Kazakhstan Nursultan Nazarbayev Nazarbayev Nursultan .

Vladimir Putin and Nursultan Nazarbayev approved a plan of joint actions of Russia and Kazakhstan for 2013–2015. A memorandum between the Government of the Russian Federation and the Government of the Republic of Kazakhstan on cooperation in setting up an emergency response system with the use of ERA-GLONASS system and possible development of additional transport navigation and information services on its basis was signed in the presence of the two leaders.
