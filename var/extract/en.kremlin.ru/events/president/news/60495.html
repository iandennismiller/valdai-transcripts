The President took part in the Immortal Regiment march

May 9, 2019
15:30
Red Square, Moscow

For the fifth time Vladimir Putin joined the Immortal Regiment march on Victory Day. The President walked through Red Square carrying a portrait of his father, a veteran of the Great Patriotic War.

On May 9, people in Russia and abroad join the Immortal Regiment march, carrying portraits of their relatives who participated in the Great Patriotic War.
